Thought Record Worksheet

  1. Write down the event or unhelpful behavior that triggered the automatic thought.
  2. Write the negative automatic thoughts you had with how much you believe the thoughts.
  3. Write which emotions were felt before, during, or after the event and include how intense (0-100%) the emotion was
  4. Come up with an adaptive response. And adaptive response is an alternative way of reacting to the intitial event. There are questions below that can help you with this box.
  5. In the outcome column, write how much you believe the automatic thought, what emotions you now feel and how intense those feelings are, and what course of action you want to take now.
